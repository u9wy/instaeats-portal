Like — Animation + Transition Specification
TIER 1🤌 "Kiss the Chef" — 2 rapid taps
🎬

Entry: Spring pop from scale 0.2 → 1.15 (overshoot) → 1.0. Slight rotation wobble. 450ms.

⏸

Hold: Gentle breathing loop ±4%. Persists until next tier or 1s idle timeout.

📳

Haptic: Single medium impact.

TRANSITIONLike T1 → T2
💨

Out (🤌): Rockets upward off-screen (translateY –140%), shrinks and rotates 20°. 250ms, fast ease-out. No fade — physically knocked aside.

💥

In (🫠): Detonates from scale 0 → 1.35 overshoot → 0.94 → 1.0. 80ms overlap with outgoing. 500ms.

📳

Haptic: Heavy × 1 + 3 rapid light pulses at 40ms intervals.

TIER 2🫠 "The Melt" — 5+ rapid taps
⏸

Hold: Slow side-sway (±2° rotation) with a downward drip-bob (±4px). Loops.

📳

Haptic: Heavy × 1, then 3 light pulses at 80ms intervals.

TRANSITIONLike T2 → T3
🫗

Out (🫠): Squashes into floor (scaleX 1.2, scaleY 0.6) then deflates off the bottom edge. 300ms. Direction is down — signals T3 is a heavier force.

💣

In (🤯): Explodes to 1.6× (largest overshoot), pinballs: 0.9 → 1.2 → 0.95 → 1.0. 100ms overlap. 500ms.

📳

Haptic: Long heavy rumble (200ms) → dramatic silence (60ms) → small thud.

TIER 3🤯 "Mind Blown" — 8+ taps, power bar
👆

Per-tap: Micro-pulse (scale 1.0→1.14→1.0), shockwave ring, sparkles. Power bar fills 0→100% over taps 8–15.

📳

Haptic escalation: Medium → Heavy → Rapid heavy → Max sustained rumble as bar fills.

Dislike — Long Press Specification
D-TIER 1😒 "Unbothered" — 0.6s hold
⏳

Charge (0–3s): A sickly green ring draws around the post image as you hold. The ring fills clockwise. The post subtly desaturates. At 3s the ring completes and pulses.

🎬

Entry: Slides up from below the frame with a slight overshoot and a dismissive head-tilt lean (rotate –5° → 0°). 500ms spring entry.

⏸

Hold: Slow contemptuous side-to-side head shake (±3° rotation, ±2px vertical bob). Loops.

🚪

Exit: Slumps and sinks off the bottom of the frame. Directional opposite of like exits.

📳

Haptic: Slow rhythmic pulse during hold (1 light buzz per second). At 3s trigger: 2 medium impacts with 80ms gap — a distinct "registered" signal.

TRANSITIONDislike D1 → D2
🤢

Out (😒 slumps): The grimace emoji squashes sideways and drops off-frame to the left with a –10° rotate, as if losing composure. 300ms.

💀

In (🤢 lurches): Enters from scale 0 with a rotation overshoot suggesting a heave — 1.3× → 0.92× → 1.0×. Ring changes to orange. 400ms. Screen flashes orange briefly.

📳

Haptic: 3 medium impacts in quick succession (50ms apart) — mimics the sensation of a stomach lurch.

D-TIER 2🤢 "Nauseated" — 1.2s hold
⏸

Hold: Heaving animation — bobs up and lurches forward repeatedly (scale pulse + translate). Rotation ±4°. Cycle: 1.4s. The post background bleeds to a sickly orange-green tint.

📳

Haptic: Rhythmic double-buzz every 1.2s (mimics heave rhythm). Each buzz: light + medium 60ms apart.

TRANSITIONDislike D2 → D3
💀

Out (🤢 retches down): Lurches sideways, scaleX widens to 1.1 and scaleY crushes, then falls off the bottom-right corner with a –20° spin. 350ms.

🤮

In (🤮 detonates): Explodes from center at 1.7× scale (largest dislike overshoot), multi-bounce landing: 0.85 → 1.15 → 0.97 → 1.0. Rotation swings –15° → +8° → –3° → 0°. Screen flash: red. 550ms.

📳

Haptic: Single long heavy rumble (250ms) — the heaviest single hit in the entire system. Followed by 200ms silence.

D-TIER 3🤮 "Full Disgust" — 2s hold, rage bar
⏸

Hold: Continuous convulsive shudder — rapid micro-translations (±2px) and rotation oscillations (±3°) at ~0.9s cycle. Communicates barely-contained revulsion.

📊

Rage bar: A red power bar fills as the hold continues past 8s (full at 15s). Labels: "Can't look…" → "Getting worse…" → "Pure disgust…" → "ABSOLUTE REVULSION!!"

🌑

Post tint: Screen vignette darkens to red around edges as bar fills.

📳

Haptic escalation: Rapid light buzzes (every 300ms) → medium buzz every 200ms → heavy every 150ms → at max: long sustained double-heavy rumble [150,50,200]ms.
